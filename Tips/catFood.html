Healthy Food Choices for Your Cat

Cats need a balanced diet containing proteins, vitamins, and essential nutrients. Always offer high-quality cat food and avoid giving foods like chocolate, onion, or dairy products as they can be harmful. Provide fresh water daily, include occasional treats, and ensure your cat’s meals match their age and activity level. A healthy diet keeps them active and happy.

Recommended Foods :

  • High-quality dry or wet cat food.
  • Protein sources like chicken, turkey, or fish (fully cooked).
  • Occasional cat-friendly treats.
  • Plenty of fresh, clean water daily.

Foods to Avoid :

  • Chocolate, milk, onions, garlic.
  • Human snacks (chips, biscuits).
  • Raw meat or raw eggs.

Additional Tips :

  • Choose food based on age: kitten, adult, or senior.
  • Feed small portions multiple times a day.
  • Consult a vet if your cat is overweight or underweight.
