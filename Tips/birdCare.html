Bird Care Basics for Beginners

Birds require a clean and spacious cage where they can move freely. Place perches at different heights and add toys to keep them mentally active. Feed them a mix of seeds, fruits, and vegetables. Clean the cage regularly to prevent infections and ensure they get natural sunlight or full-spectrum lighting. Birds thrive in a calm, safe environment.

Basic Care Steps :

  • Provide a large cage with enough flying space.
  • Add perches at different heights and materials.
  • Offer toys like bells, ropes, and wooden blocks.
  • Feed a mix of seeds, fruits, and vegetables.
  • Clean water daily and change cage liners regularly.

Additional Tips :

  • Ensure they get natural sunlight or full-spectrum light.
  • Avoid using scented candles or aerosols near birds.
  • Allow supervised out-of-cage time in a safe room.
